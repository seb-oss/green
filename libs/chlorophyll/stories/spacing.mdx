# Spacing

Design tokens for spacing (paddings and margins) within and between components. Eg. a button consists of it's text height 16 (font-size), spacing value eg. top and bottom of spacing value 4 (12px / 0.75rem) and a border of 1 px (16 + 12 + 12 + 1 + 1 = 42). By relying on tokens for spacing we simplify and make our design more concise at the same time.
8 levels could and probably should be slimmed down to maybe 6 (remove 4 and 6?).
